Noodles

This page provides various information and insights regarding instant noodle consumption from certain countries around the world.

Noodle Topic 1

What countries consume the most instant noodles per capita and how does instant noodle consumption change between 2018 and 2022?

    1. China clearly consumes the most instant noodles per capita, and most countries in Asia also consume instant noodles at a high rate.
    2. Brazil and India consume instant noodles at higher rates as the years move from 2018 to 2022.
    3. The United States consumes the highest rate of instant noodles per capita outside of countries in Asia, and European countries consume the lowest rate of instant noodles per capita.

Noodle Topic 2

How does average noodle consumption per capita differ by continent?

    1. On average, Asian countries consume roughly 4000 instant noodles per capita whereas most others are less than 1000 instant noodles per capita, indicating that instant noodle consumption is highest by far in Asia.
    2. The Americas have the second largest average consumption, only behind Asia while Europe and Oceania consume relatively less instant noodles per capita, which may suggest different lifestyles or cultural differences at play in these countries.
    3. The average rate of instant noodle consumption for each continent increased from 2018 to 2022 as demonstrated by the incline in each bar graph.

Noodle Topic 3

What was the percent change in noodle consumption per capita by country?

    1. Turkey has by far the highest percent change with 425% growing noodle consumption per capita from 80 in 2018 to 420 in 2022.

    2. Singapore, Costa Rica, Denmark and Argentina all had 0% change.

    3. The average percent change for the countries in this data is 45.82%.
