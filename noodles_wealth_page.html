Noodles x Wealth

Have you ever wondered how instant noodle consumption relates to wealth?

This page provides various information and insights regarding instant noodle consumption and its relationship to wealth-related metrics.

Wealth Topic 1

How do GDP and noodle consumption per capita change from 2018 to 2022?

    1. Average GDP per capita hit its lowest point in 2020, which may be associated with the Covid-19 pandemic.
    2. Average noodle consumption increased heavily from 2019 to 2022 as seen by the sharp increase in the line.
    3. Average GDP per capita by year increased sharply from 2021 to 2022, and instant noodle consumption also increased, but the increase in the average noodle consumption was a much smaller percent change than for average GDP per capita.

Wealth Topic 2

How do average GDP per capita and noodle consumption for each continent change from 2018 to 2022?

    1. Average GDP per capita increased for all continents across the 2018-2022 time period with roughly similar line patterns for each.
    2. Oceania and Europe have the highest GDP per capita, and the lowest noodle consumption across the time frame.
    3. Asia has the highest average noodle consumption, and the second lowest GDP per capita.
